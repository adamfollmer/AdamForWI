Your Simple Role

Connect and Listen

1

Watch the Weekly Video

Each week, I will share a video that breaks down one key challenge facing our country and the implications on working families. The goal is to give you the confidence to talk about these issues with other people.

2

Talk to Nine People

Commit to finding 9 other committed people you already know and trust. Aim to connect with three people in each of these three circles:

  • Work / School
  • Friends / Family
  • Neighbors
3

Listen and Share

While connecting with your network, listen for ideas that connect back to the videos. Offer a real, authentic alternative to the squeeze people are feeling whether it's related to their time, their wallet, or their well-being.

We are looking for 300 core people across every working-class sector (healthcare, education, laborers, public sector workers, and more) to be the first link in this campaign.

We are building our communication system based on genuine trust and interpersonal connections. These 300 core organizers will launch our invitation-only network by personally inviting 9 people they know and trust, who will keep the process growing. This method of expanding through trusted, in-person invitations ensures our growth is democratic, effective, and protective of your privacy.

Why This Process?

  • The campaign stays connected to working-class issues.
  • Everyone has a voice while protecting your privacy.
