Enter the Town Hall

How It Works

1

Join the Digital Town Hall

Get connected with someone in our campaign to sign up for our community platform. The platform is meant to be a semi-anonymous, transparent, and accessible digital town hall. Read more about the platform by looking at the "Frequently Asked Questions" via the link below.

community.adamforwi.com
2

Find Your Nine

Have real, face-to-face conversations with nine people across three spaces in your life. You don’t need to be a policy expert, the campaign will give you the tools you need to communicate these political issues that impact all our lives. Your role is to be the translator for your circle.

  • ● Labor/Learning Your coworkers, fellow students, or trade partners.
  • ● Community/Connection Your friends, family, faith circles, community groups, or hobby spaces.
  • ● Neighbors The people in your direct geographic community.
3

Grow the Network

When your nine people are ready, invite them onto the platform. They find their nine. The goal is to get the network to grow through trust and direct connections. The digital town hall acts as a way of giving a voice no matter when they become involved. Information and communication flows in every direction, keeping the whole community responsive and connected.
