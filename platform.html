Restore Democratic Integrity

Remove Money from Politics Ban all corporate PAC and lobbyist donations to candidates and implement a system of equal and fair candidate access.

Mandate Single-Issue Legislation End massive, unreadable bills. Require every piece of legislation to address only one subject for transparent, accountable voting.

Establish Term Limits Enact term limits for both the House and Senate to end careerism and increase opportunity for new voices.

Pro-Worker Tax Reform Implement a wealth tax on fortunes over $50 million, close corporate tax loopholes, and ensure the wealthiest Americans pay their fair share.

Adopt Ranked-Choice Voting Encourage positive campaigning and make every vote count in every race.

Lift the Common Class

Guarantee Healthcare as a Right Implement a single-payer healthcare system to ensure universal coverage.

Tuition-Free Higher Education Make public colleges, universities, and trade schools tuition-free.

Support Working Families Guarantee universal childcare, paid parental leave, and a 32-hour workweek with no loss in pay to improve quality of life.

Ensure Housing for All Massively expand public and affordable housing options and provide rental assistance to end homelessness and housing insecurity.

Invest in the Future

Reskill the American Workforce Launch a national program to provide free training for jobs in modern infrastructure, healthcare, education, and technology.

Rebuild America's Infrastructure Modernize our transportation, power grid, and water systems with a focus on climate resilience and green technology.

Reimagine Public Education Fully fund our K-12 schools, increase teacher pay, and invest in programs for our future problems.

Promote Open Innovation Require all publicly funded research to be open access and invest in public digital libraries and tools to foster a more informed society.
