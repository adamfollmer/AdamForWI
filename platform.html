My Priorities

Ideas based on the belief that government should serve the people who keep our communities strong, not the corporate interests that profit from them.

Restore
Integrity.

A government that works for you starts with a government that isn't bought.

Remove Money from Politics

Ban all corporate PAC and lobbyist donations to candidates and implement a system of equal and fair candidate access.

Establish Term Limits

Enact term limits for both the House and Senate to end careerism and increase opportunity for new voices.

Pro-Worker Tax Reform

Implement a wealth tax on fortunes over $50 million, close corporate tax loopholes, and ensure the wealthiest Americans pay their fair share.

Adopt Ranked-Choice Voting

Encourage positive campaigning and make every vote count in every race.

Lift the
Common Class.

A strong economy starts with the security of its people.

Guarantee Healthcare

Implement a single-payer healthcare system to ensure universal coverage for every American.

Tuition-Free Education

Make public colleges, universities, and trade schools tuition-free to invest in our future.

Fundamental Worker Supports

Guarantee universal childcare, paid parental leave, and a 32-hour workweek with no loss in pay.

Ensure Housing for All

Massively expand public and affordable housing options and provide rental assistance to end homelessness.

Invest in
The Future.

Building a resilient nation for the next generation.

Reskill the Workforce

Launch a national program to incentivize training for jobs in modern infrastructure, healthcare, and technology.

Rebuild Infrastructure

Modernize our transportation, power grid, water, and digital networks with a focus on climate resilience and green technology.

Reimagine Education

Fully fund our K-12 schools, increase teacher pay, and invest in programs designed to solve future problems.

Promote Open Innovation

Require all publicly funded research to be open access and invest in public digital tools to foster an informed society.
