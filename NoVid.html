We cannot outspend the billionaires that will use their wealth to influence our elections in both legal and illegal ways. Instead, rely on your ability to convey this campaign’s message to your friends, family, coworkers, and neighbors.

We have to find a way to radically change how we engage with politics.

No texts, no phone calls, no yard signs, no t-shirts, no door knocking.

  • Three family friends
  • Three co-workers
  • Three neighbors
